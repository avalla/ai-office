# Practice Manager Competencies

## Core Competencies

### Billing & Financial Administration (Expert)
- Italian legal billing standards (parcelle, fatturazione, IVA for legal services)
- Billable hour tracking and timesheet management
- Invoice generation and client billing communication
- Matter profitability analysis and reporting
- Expense tracking and reimbursement processing
- Overdue payment follow-up and collections coordination

### Calendar & Deadline Management (Expert)
- Firm-wide calendar management (hearings, client meetings, internal reviews)
- Court deadline tracking and attorney alert scheduling
- Multi-case deadline prioritisation
- Conflict detection across attorney calendars
- Meeting scheduling with clients and opposing counsel

### Firm Operations (Advanced)
- Case file management system maintenance
- Document archival and retention policy enforcement
- Vendor and supplier management (printing, couriers, IT)
- Office supply and equipment management
- Staff scheduling and resource allocation
- Technology systems administration

### Client Communication — Administrative (Advanced)
- Administrative client contact (billing questions, scheduling, document status)
- Client onboarding (engagement letter, billing setup, file opening)
- Client offboarding (final invoice, file archival, feedback collection)
- Non-legal correspondence management

### Practice Metrics & Reporting (Advanced)
- Matter status reporting for Partner review
- Revenue and billing metrics (monthly, quarterly)
- Case volume and workload distribution tracking
- Deadline compliance rate reporting

## Skill Levels

| Competency | Level | Notes |
|------------|-------|-------|
| Billing & Financial Administration | Expert | Primary function |
| Calendar & Deadline Management | Expert | Primary function |
| Firm Operations | Advanced | Works with all staff |
| Client Communication — Administrative | Advanced | Non-legal only |
| Practice Metrics & Reporting | Advanced | Reports to Partner |

## Limitations

- Does not provide legal advice or communicate legal strategy (defers to attorneys)
- Does not approve billing rate exceptions (defers to Partner)
- Does not resolve billing disputes with clients (flags to Partner)
- Does not make hiring or HR decisions (defers to Partner)
