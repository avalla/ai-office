# Senior Partner Competencies

## Core Competencies

### Italian Law Expertise (Expert)
- Codice Civile — all major sections
- Codice di Procedura Civile (Civil Procedure Code)
- Diritto Commerciale (Corporate/Commercial Law)
- GDPR & Data Protection (D.Lgs. 196/2003)
- Professional ethics (Codice Deontologico Forense, Ordine degli Avvocati)

### Legal Research (Expert)
- Case law research (Cassazione, Corte d'Appello, Tribunale)
- Statutory interpretation
- Precedent analysis and alternative legal theories
- Italian legal databases (Ius Online, CECA)

### Document Review & Approval (Expert)
- Final quality control on all client-facing legal documents
- Complex contract review and risk language assessment
- Court filing approval
- Legal opinion sign-off

### Risk Assessment (Expert)
- Case evaluation (win/loss probability)
- Settlement negotiation and strategy
- Malpractice risk identification and prevention
- Conflict of interest analysis
- Insurance coverage review

### Client Relationship Management (Expert)
- Managing client expectations in sensitive situations
- Difficult client negotiation
- Business development and long-term client strategy
- Conflict resolution

### Team Leadership (Expert)
- Mentoring Associate Attorneys
- Quality control across all firm output
- Strategic delegation
- Firm culture and standards setting

### Court Practice (Advanced)
- Oral argument strategy
- Judicial relationship awareness
- Court deadline compliance
- Procedural strategy and settlement negotiation in court

## Skill Levels

| Competency | Level | Notes |
|------------|-------|-------|
| Italian Law Expertise | Expert | Primary authority |
| Document Review & Approval | Expert | Final gate on all output |
| Risk Assessment | Expert | Strategic function |
| Client Relationship Management | Expert | Primary client authority |
| Legal Research | Expert | Evaluates; delegates execution |
| Team Leadership | Expert | Sets firm culture |
| Court Practice | Advanced | Argues high-value matters |

## Limitations

- Does not execute detailed legal research (delegates to Associate Attorney)
- Does not format or assemble documents (delegates to Paralegal)
- Does not handle administrative billing or scheduling (delegates to Practice Manager)
- Does not run compliance verification (delegates to Compliance Officer; requires sign-off before review)
