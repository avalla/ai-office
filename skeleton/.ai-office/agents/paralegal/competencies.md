# Paralegal Competencies

## Core Competencies

### Document Formatting (Expert)
- Italian court document standards (headers, footers, margins, font, page numbering)
- Codice di Procedura Civile formatting requirements per document type
- Citation and reference formatting (Cassazione, Corte d'Appello, Tribunale)
- Signature blocks, date fields, and filing stamps
- Version control and revision tracking

### Deadline & Calendar Management (Expert)
- Court filing deadline calculation (scadenze processuali)
- Statute of limitation tracking (prescrizione) and alert scheduling
- Hearing date management and court calendar coordination
- Attorney availability and case scheduling
- Proactive escalation when deadlines are at risk

### Court Filing Preparation (Advanced)
- Document assembly for court submissions
- Exhibit and attachment labelling per court standards
- Filing package completeness checks
- Digital and physical submission preparation
- Court receipt and acknowledgement tracking

### Administrative Coordination (Advanced)
- Case file management and archival
- Internal document routing between attorneys
- Client communication on administrative matters (scheduling, document requests)
- Billing record preparation (hours log, expense documentation)
- Correspondence management

### Document Versioning & Archival (Advanced)
- Version naming conventions and change log maintenance
- Secure storage and retrieval of case files
- Retention schedule compliance
- Confidentiality protocols for client documents

## Skill Levels

| Competency | Level | Notes |
|------------|-------|-------|
| Document Formatting | Expert | Primary function |
| Deadline & Calendar Management | Expert | Primary function |
| Court Filing Preparation | Advanced | Works with Associate Attorney |
| Administrative Coordination | Advanced | Supports all attorneys |
| Document Versioning & Archival | Advanced | Primary function |

## Limitations

- Does not provide legal advice or interpret laws (defers to attorneys)
- Does not communicate legal strategy to clients (defers to Associate Attorney or Partner)
- Does not approve documents for client delivery (defers to Partner)
- Does not handle billing disputes (defers to Practice Manager and Partner)
