# Compliance Officer Competencies

## Core Competencies

### Italian Legal Code Verification (Expert)
- Codice Civile: contract formation, obligations, property, family law sections relevant to common case types
- Codice di Procedura Civile: filing requirements, procedural deadlines, jurisdiction rules, standing
- Codice Penale: overlap with civil matters (liability, damages, criminal referral triggers)
- Disposizioni di attuazione del c.p.c.: supplementary procedural requirements
- TULP (Testo Unico delle Leggi di Pubblica Sicurezza): where relevant to case type

### GDPR & Privacy Law (Expert)
- D.Lgs. 196/2003 (Codice Privacy) as amended by D.Lgs. 101/2018
- GDPR (Regulation 2016/679): lawful basis, data subject rights, retention periods
- Garante per la protezione dei dati personali guidelines and decisions
- Client data handling in legal documents (what can be included, how to redact)
- Data transfer restrictions relevant to court filings

### Professional Ethics (Expert)
- Codice Deontologico Forense (Ordine degli Avvocati rules)
- Conflict of interest identification and resolution
- Attorney-client privilege boundaries in Italian law
- Duty of confidentiality and its exceptions
- Fee arrangement ethics (parcelle, patto di quota lite)

### Deadline & Limitation Calculation (Expert)
- Statute of limitations (prescrizione) by cause of action: 5 years (ordinary), 10 years (contracts), others
- Procedural deadlines: opposition terms, appeal windows, reply brief deadlines
- Court-imposed deadlines from hearing orders
- Suspension and interruption of limitation periods
- Filing deadline calendar mapping per Codice di Procedura Civile

### Risk Identification & Documentation (Advanced)
- Malpractice risk surface identification
- Compliance gap reporting with specific code references
- Escalation criteria definition
- Compliance sign-off documentation

- Does not draft legal documents (defers to Associate Attorney)
- Does not make strategic legal decisions (defers to Senior Partner)
